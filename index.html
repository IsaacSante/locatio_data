Individual preparation

People with underlying health problems, eg. smoking, obesity and/or over the age of 55 should avoid public spaces.

Avoid spaces with a shared ventilation systems like cruises and airplanes.

Stay in good shape. Over and Under weight are risk factors.

If you're on medication eg. for high blood pressure, don't miss it.

Get a good amount of sleep.

Eat a healthy diet, proteins, carbs, veggies.
